The Soul Behind Afeli Studios

Afeli Studios was founded in Mombasa, Coast, Kenya - a place where art, struggle, and nature live side by side. We believe in the power of creativity to transform lives - to teach, to heal, and to inspire. Our mission is to uplift young artists, storytellers, and dreamers from underrepresented communities -

giving them the tools and confidence to create, express, and build their own futures. We embrace raw expression - the messy, honest, unfiltered kind of art that comes from the streets, the soil, and the soul. Here, imperfection is power. Here, every brushstroke tells a story of resistance and rebirth.

We don’t paint for perfection. We paint for purpose. Felix Odhiambo
